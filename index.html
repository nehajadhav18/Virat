VIRAT KOHLI

Virat Kohli is An International Cricketer

Virat Kohli born 5 November 1988 and former captain of the Indian national team who plays as a right-handed batsman for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the IPL and for Delhi in Indian domestic cricket. Widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.

Domestic career

  • In July 2006, Kohli was selected in the India Under-19 squad on its tour of England.
  • In 18 August 2008, Kohli was selected for inclusion in the ODl squad for the tour of Sri Lanka and the Champions Trophy in Pakistan.
  • In 20 June 2011, Kohli was selected for Test cricket for tour of West Indies
  • In 12 June 2010, Kohli was selected for T20 for Zimbabwe tour

Breakthrough

  1. 2012–2013: ODI ascendancy and ascension to vice-captaincy.
  2. 2014: T20 World Cup and assuming Test-captaincy.
  3. 2015–2016: World Cups and limited-over success.
  4. 2017–2018: Dominant batting and leadership.
  5. 2019–2020: Record breaking captaincy and batting woes.
  6. 2021–2022: Captaincy exit.
